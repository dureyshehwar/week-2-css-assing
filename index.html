A Trip to Pakistan

This region is known for its stunning landscapes, which include snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and verdant valleys. Pakistan's Northern Areas are a natural wonder and a cultural hub with a rich heritage and friendly people.

Things to Explore and See:

  • Visit the Pakistan Northern areas
  • Explore the Himalayas, Karakoram and the Hindu kush mountain ranges, containing many of the highest peaks in the world a
  • Take a Hike in Kumrat Valley Forest is a place that will capture your heart and soul
  • See a mesmerizing Match play which is called POLO.
